How To Make A Sandwich

  1. First, grab two slices of bread.
  2. Second, slather mayo or mustard or other condiments on your slices of bread.
  3. Third, layer your cheese, meat, lettuce, and tomato - as desired on one of the slices.
  4. Fourth, put the slice of bread without the items mentioned in step three on top, above the sandwhich contents.
  5. Fifth, use a knife and cut the completed sandwich down the center - creating two halves.
